Grid System


There are different elements in the grid system. The grid system on the index page is controlled of having a section that keeps control of the three info boxes, while the the grid system on the subsides are two boxes, controlled by a section. To make space between the different tiles, there are vertical spacers and horizontal spacers. Basically, if you have a section, you have perhaps two pieces of content, serperated by a vertical spacers. The next section is seperated from the previous one with a horizontal spacer.

An article (written content) has flex3, while image has flex 1, giving the article 3/4 of the space in a tile section, while the image has 1/4.
